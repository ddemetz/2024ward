RepreZent is a category of Gen-Z for Change’s 2024 endorsements that highlights young people running to promote, or represent, the interests of Gen-Z in office. Each candidate in this program will be under age of 30 on their respective inauguration day, and has demonstrated to our organization they share critical values and beliefs that align best with those of our generation. Moreover, given that the outcomes of this election cycle stand to deeply impact young people’s futures, we thought it was important to take the opportunity to highlight progressive young candidates we feel will work to create the policy and change so desperately needed in the coming years.
